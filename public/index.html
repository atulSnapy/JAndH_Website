About us

What is a home? It’s a cromo(colour) where every little thing is a reflection of you. It’s a space, you make yours by adding pieces of memories, laughter and togetherness. But beyond everything, what you find in your space is the comfort. And it’s this comfort that makes you feel at home.

Cromoscape, a brand from the world of J AND H TEXTILES weaves contemporary and traditional designs on bed, bath and coordinates. Our products are not only elegant, eclectic and eccentric, but are also thoughtfully crafted to make your experience of living a comfortable lifestyle, more comfortable.

With thoughtful living at heart, we create products to give you an experience. And how do we do it? We do it with love, care and innovation to make better, better.

We welcome you to our world of thoughtful living that is more yours than ours
